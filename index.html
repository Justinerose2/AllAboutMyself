About Me

Hi, Let me introduce you myself, I am Justine Rose Casas, at 21 years old I am a home based nailtech here at nasipit. I'm a passionate and skilled nail technician dedicated to bringing beauty and style to your fingertips. With a keen eye for detail and a creative touch, I specialize in providing a wide range of nail services to enhance your natural beauty or express your unique personality.

From classic manicures and pedicures to intricate nail art designs, I take pride in staying up-to-date with the latest trends, techniques, and high-quality products. Whether you're looking for a simple and elegant look or something bold and vibrant, I am committed to delivering personalized and professional nail care that exceeds your expectations.

I strive to create a relaxing and enjoyable experience for my clients, ensuring not only aesthetically pleasing results but also a moment of self-care and pampering. Let me transform your nails into a work of art, and together, we can make your hands and feet the ultimate expression of your style and individuality.
